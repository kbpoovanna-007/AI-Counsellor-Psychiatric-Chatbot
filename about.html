MindWave: AI Allies for Students

Welcome to our AI-powered website, a heartfelt response to the concerning rise in student suicides, particularly in places like the Koto Factory and wider India. Our platform offers a safe haven for students to freely express themselves, find solace, and receive guidance.


At the core of our initiative is an AI counselor, a compassionate listener trained to provide considerate and supportive responses, serving as an initial step for users to open up. Beyond this, our groundbreaking feature introduces an AI family including a caring AI father, mother, and others, collectively providing holistic and understanding support.


We prioritize privacy, ensuring that all interactions remain confidential and secure. Our vision is to lessen the stigma surrounding mental health, making support universally accessible.


Join us in creating a world where everyone can seek the help they deserve, guided by innovative AI companions and fueled by shared empathy. Together, we can make a difference.
